# Welcome to Bitnex

Bitnex is a non-custodial trading interface for perpetual futures and spot markets, built on top of Hyperliquid — a decentralized, on-chain exchange. Bitnex gives you a fast, clean way to trade, while Hyperliquid provides the liquidity, the matching engine, and custody of your funds. That means your assets never leave your control: there's no traditional sign-up and no KYC on Bitnex, and once you're set up, trading is gasless.

## What you can do

- **Trade perps and spot** — go long or short on perpetual futures with leverage, or trade spot markets.
- **One-click trading** — enable an agent wallet once and place orders without a wallet popup on every trade.
- **Keep custody of your funds** — your USDC stays in Hyperliquid's on-chain system, never with Bitnex.
- **More than trading** — explore Portfolio, Vaults/Earn, Staking, Referrals, and Swap, in a simple Lite view or the full Pro view.
